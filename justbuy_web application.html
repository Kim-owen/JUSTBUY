Payment Architecture

How JustBuy Payments Work

End-to-end flow powered by the Korba Xchange API — from product selection to order fulfilment.

1

Select & Checkout

Choose airtime, data, or a utility. Review order details and select Mobile Money as payment method.

2

JustBuy Initiates

Backend generates a unique transaction_id, signs with HMAC auth, and calls the Korba /collect endpoint.

3

You Approve on Phone

Korba sends a MoMo prompt directly to your handset. Approve the payment on MTN, Vodafone, or AirtelTigo.

4

Callback Confirms

Korba fires a callback to JustBuy's HTTPS endpoint with SUCCESS or FAILED — this is the single source of truth.

🔁

Built-in Fallback & Reconciliation

If a callback is not received, JustBuy automatically polls the Korba transaction status endpoint to reconcile. If payment succeeds but delivery fails, funds are instantly credited to your JustBuy Wallet — you never lose money.

Security

Production-Grade Security

JustBuy is built to enterprise security standards — licensed, regulated, and fully auditable.

🔐

HMAC Authentication

Every API request to Korba Xchange is signed with HMAC authentication. Production keys are enforced with IP whitelisting on all backend services.

🪪

KYC Enforcement

Wallet access is gated behind KYC verification via Ghana Card. States are tracked: NOT_SUBMITTED → PENDING → VERIFIED / REJECTED.

📋

Full Audit Trail

Every transaction, callback, wallet operation, and admin action is logged immutably. Daily reconciliation ensures consistency between JustBuy and Korba records.

🔔

Idempotent Transactions

Each payment uses a unique transaction_id. Duplicate requests are safely rejected. Callbacks are the authoritative source of truth.

Payment Architecture

From Checkout to Fulfilment

The complete end-to-end JustBuy payment journey — powered by Korba Xchange API and built for production reliability.

System Actors

Who's Involved in Every Payment

Five actors work together to process every JustBuy transaction seamlessly.

👤

Customer

Initiates the purchase on the JustBuy app or USSD, and approves the MoMo prompt on their phone.

📱

JustBuy Application

The frontend — mobile app, web platform, or USSD — where the customer selects products and checks out.

⚙️

JustBuy Backend

Generates unique transaction_ids, signs requests with HMAC, and processes callbacks from Korba.

🔗

Korba Xchange API

Payment gateway that connects JustBuy to mobile networks, processes prompts, and fires callbacks on completion.

📶

Mobile Money Networks

MTN MoMo, Vodafone Cash, and AirtelTigo — the networks that debit the customer and confirm the payment.

👛

JustBuy Wallet

Each KYC-verified customer has a wallet that stores credits, refunds, and failed-purchase funds for reuse.

Step by Step

The Complete Payment Journey

Eight precise steps from product selection to order fulfilment — nothing left to chance.

1

Product Selection

The customer visits JustBuy, selects a product or service (airtime, data bundle, utility), and proceeds to checkout.

2

Checkout & Payment Method

The customer reviews the order and selects Mobile Money as the payment method. Supported: MTN, Vodafone, AirtelTigo.

3

Payment Initialization

JustBuy backend generates a unique transaction_id, signs with HMAC, and calls the Korba /collect endpoint.

4

Customer Authorization

Korba sends a Mobile Money prompt directly to the customer's handset. The customer approves (or declines) the payment.

5

Payment Processing (Pending State)

Korba processes the transaction with the selected Mobile Money network. Remains in PENDING state until the network confirms.

6

Callback Confirmation ⭐ Source of Truth

Korba fires a callback containing transaction_id and status: SUCCESS | FAILED. This callback is the single authoritative source of truth.

7

Order Fulfilment (SUCCESS)

JustBuy marks the order as PAID and fulfils the service — airtime topped up, data activated, or bill paid instantly.

8

Failed Purchase Credit (Safety Net)

If payment succeeds but service delivery fails, funds are immediately credited to the customer's JustBuy Wallet. Zero financial loss to the customer.

Business Rules

USSD Business Rules

The core rules that govern every USSD interaction on the JustBuy platform.

📱

Mobile Money Only

JustBuy USSD supports Mobile Money payments only. Card payments are not available on the USSD channel.

🪪

Wallet Requires KYC

Wallet access via USSD is restricted to KYC-verified users only. Guest and unverified users cannot access wallet features.

💳

Failed Purchase Credits

If a payment succeeds but service delivery fails, funds are credited to the customer's JustBuy Wallet automatically.

🔁

Callback is Truth

All USSD transaction outcomes are determined by Korba callbacks. Transactions remain PENDING until a callback confirms SUCCESS or FAILED.
